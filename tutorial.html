Tutorial

Here's a snippet of a hello world.

"Hello world!"

Simple, huh. Infact this just prints Hello world in the top left corner because we haven't added any coordinates to the snippet. But it still works;

"Hello world!"( 5, 70 )

This will place the snippet onto the x of 5 and the y of 70. The default coordinates are 0, 0
